Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the purpose of this website?

A: This website allows members of the public to report the location and condition of shopping trolleys they believe have been abandoned. The goal is to create a publicly viewable log of these reports.

Q: Is this an official service run by the supermarkets or the local council?

A: No. This is an independent, unofficial service. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Coles, Woolworths, Aldi, IGA, Kmart, Big W, Bunnings, any other retailer, or any local council.

Q: Does reporting a trolley here guarantee it will be collected?

A: No. Submitting a report here simply adds it to the public log on this website. It does not automatically notify the trolley owner or collection services. For official collection, you usually need to contact the relevant retailer directly or use their specific reporting channels (like Trolley Tracker, if applicable).

Q: What information is collected when I make a report?

A: We collect the location (latitude/longitude), timestamp, selected trolley brand(s), selected condition(s), and any comments you add. Your IP address is also logged by the server for security. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: Is the information I submit made public?

A: Yes. The location, timestamp, brand(s), condition(s), and comments you submit are displayed on the public "See Logs" page. Your IP address is not displayed publicly. Do not submit any information you aren't comfortable sharing publicly.

Q: How accurate is the location shown on the map?

A: The location accuracy depends entirely on your device's GPS signal and settings at the time you press the "Get Date/Time & Location" button. We cannot guarantee the accuracy.

Q: Can I remove a log I submitted?

A: There is no automated way to remove logs. If you submitted a log containing sensitive personal information by mistake, please contact us via [Your Contact Method] with details of the log (e.g., approximate time and location), and we will review the request. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Q: Who do I contact if I have questions or issues?

A: Please contact us via info@sandgroper.net .
